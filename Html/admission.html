Undergraduate Admission

CCN-UST seeks to enroll a talented and diverse class of undergraduates who will learn to think critically, engage with others, and contribute to the betterment of society. We are committed to a holistic review process that considers each applicant's unique experiences, achievements, and potential to thrive at CCN-UST.

Graduate Admission

CCN-UST offers a range of graduate programs designed to prepare students for advanced careers in their chosen fields. Our graduate admissions process is rigorous and selective, ensuring that we admit students who demonstrate academic excellence, research potential, and a commitment to contributing to their disciplines.
